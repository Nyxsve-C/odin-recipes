Description

Chilaquiles rojos with chicken or fried egg

Ingredients

  • Totopos
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Jalapeño pepper
  • Cilantro
  • Chicken or egg
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Cream
  • Queso fresco

Steps

  1. Roast or grill the tomatoes with the onions, garlic and jalapeño
  2. Blend
  3. Cook the chicken or the egg
  4. Serve the totopos in a bowl
  5. Serve the red sauce on the totopos
  6. Top with chopped onion, cilantro, cream and queso fresco
