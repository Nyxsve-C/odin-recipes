Maíz tortillas quesadillas

Ingredients

  • Maíz tortillas
  • Meltable cheese
  • Sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat tortillas in a comal, flipping them every 30 seconds
  2. Place cheese strands in a tortilla and fold the tortilla
  3. Heat until cheese melts
  4. Add sauce inside
